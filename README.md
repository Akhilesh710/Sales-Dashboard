# 📊 Super Store Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Description

This project analyzes Super Store sales data using Power BI to understand sales performance, profit trends, shipping patterns, and regional distribution. An interactive dashboard was built to monitor key KPIs and visualize business performance.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales, orders, and profit performance
* Identify top-performing regions and product categories
* Track monthly sales and profit trends
* Understand shipping modes and customer segments

## 🛠 Skills & Tools

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling
* DAX Measures
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence Reporting

## 📦 Deliverables

* Interactive Power BI Dashboard
* KPI Cards (Sales, Orders, Profit, Ship Days)
* Sales & Profit Trend Analysis
* Region and Segment Performance Reports
* Category & Sub-Category Sales Analysis
* Dynamic Filters for Regional Analysis

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total Sales reached **1.6M** with **22K orders**
* Technology and Office Supplies generated the highest revenue
* West region contributed the largest share of sales
* Standard Class was the most used shipping mode

## 🚀 Impact

Provided a clear view of sales performance, regional contribution, and product category trends, enabling better strategic decisions for sales growth and operational optimization.
